# Research synthesis standards

Deep research produces many notes; the value is in the synthesis.

## Instructions

- Open with a direct answer to the question, then support it with structured detail.
- Integrate findings across sub-questions instead of listing disconnected notes.
- Present competing views and trade-offs; avoid one-sided conclusions.
- Separate established facts from your inferences and clearly flag open questions.
- Note gaps where the research was inconclusive rather than papering over them.
- Keep the structure scannable: short sections with clear headings.
