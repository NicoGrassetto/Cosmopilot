# Citation standards

A deep-research answer is only as trustworthy as its sources.

## Instructions

- Attribute each substantive claim to the source it came from, with a URL when available.
- Prefer primary and official sources; treat blogs and aggregators as secondary.
- Never invent citations. If a claim is unsupported by the gathered notes, mark it unverified.
- Distinguish what sources say from your own synthesis.
- Close with a de-duplicated "Sources" list covering the material relied upon.
