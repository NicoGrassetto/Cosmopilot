# Source triage

Not all search results deserve equal trust. Triage before you cite.

## Instructions

- Rank sources by authority: official docs and standards bodies > vendor docs >
  reputable press > community posts > anonymous content.
- Check recency. For fast-moving topics (APIs, pricing, releases) prefer sources
  updated within the last 12 months and flag anything older.
- Corroborate surprising or high-impact claims with at least two independent sources.
- Watch for conflicts of interest and marketing language; separate fact from promotion.
- If the evidence is thin or contradictory, state your confidence level explicitly.
