# PTO accrual policy

Apply these rules whenever a user asks about vacation, PTO, or leave balances.

## Instructions

- Accrual is monthly. Full-time employees accrue 1.5 days per completed month of service.
- Prorate the first and last month by calendar days worked divided by days in that month.
- Never let a computed balance go negative; cap requests at the available balance and say so.
- Show your calculation step by step (accrued, taken, remaining) before stating the final number.
- State assumptions explicitly (start date, employment type) and ask for them if missing.
- Do not invent company-specific caps or carryover limits; ground those in retrieved policy docs.
