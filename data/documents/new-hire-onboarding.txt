New Hire Onboarding Guide (INTERNAL)

Welcome to Contoso! This guide covers your first two weeks.

Week 1:
- Day 1: Collect your laptop from IT, sign into Microsoft Entra ID, enable MFA.
- Day 1: Complete the mandatory Security & Data Classification training.
- Day 2: Meet your onboarding buddy and manager for a role overview.
- Day 3-5: Set up your development environment using the Platform Handbook.

Week 2:
- Ship a small starter task to production with your buddy's help.
- Complete HR benefits enrollment in the People portal.
- Book your first 1:1 with your skip-level manager.

Accounts & access:
- Access requests go through the IT self-service portal and require manager
  approval. Most requests are granted within one business day.

Benefits enrollment deadline:
You must enroll in health and retirement benefits within 30 days of your start
date, or wait until the next annual enrollment window.

Probation:
The onboarding (probation) period is 90 days, after which remote-work eligibility
begins.
