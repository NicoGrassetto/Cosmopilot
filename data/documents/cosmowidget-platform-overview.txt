CosmoWidget Platform Overview (INTERNAL)

CosmoWidget is Contoso's flagship SaaS platform for real-time tenant analytics,
built on Azure Cosmos DB.

Architecture:
- Data store: Azure Cosmos DB (NoSQL API), Session consistency by default.
- Partition key: /tenantId. Each customer is a logical tenant.
- Default container throughput: 400 RU/s, autoscaled up to 4000 RU/s.

Supported regions:
East US 2, West Europe, UK South, and Sweden Central.

Service tiers:
- Starter: up to 5 million events/month, community support.
- Business: up to 50 million events/month, 99.9% SLA, 24x5 support.
- Enterprise: unlimited events, 99.99% SLA, 24x7 support, dedicated TAM.

Data retention:
Raw events are retained for 90 days; aggregated metrics are retained for 24
months. Customers on the Enterprise tier can request custom retention.

There is no free tier for production accounts.
