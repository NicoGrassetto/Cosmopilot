Contoso Remote Work Policy (INTERNAL)

Effective date: January 2026. Owner: People Operations.

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day onboarding period are
eligible for hybrid remote work. Contractors require manager approval.

2. Core hours
Employees must be reachable between 10:00 and 15:00 in their local time zone.
Outside of core hours, schedules are flexible.

3. In-office days
Hybrid employees are expected on-site a minimum of two days per week, typically
Tuesday and Thursday, to support team collaboration.

4. Equipment
Contoso provides a laptop and one external monitor. A home-office stipend of 500
USD per year covers a desk, chair, or headset. Submit receipts through the
Expenses portal within 60 days of purchase.

5. Security
Remote workers must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems and must
keep full-disk encryption enabled. Never store customer data on personal devices.

Questions: contact peopleops@contoso.example.
