Contoso Travel & Expense Policy (INTERNAL)

Owner: Finance. Last reviewed: March 2026.

Daily meal allowance:
- Domestic travel: 75 USD per day.
- International travel: 90 USD per day.

Air travel:
- Economy class is standard for flights under 6 hours.
- Business class is permitted for flights of 6 hours or longer with director
  approval.

Hotels:
- Reimbursed up to 250 USD per night in Tier 1 cities (New York, London, Tokyo),
  180 USD elsewhere.

Submission:
- Submit expense reports within 30 days of trip completion.
- Receipts are required for any single expense above 25 USD.
- Reports are approved by your direct manager, then processed by Finance within
  10 business days.

Non-reimbursable: personal entertainment, minibar, traffic fines, and airline
seat upgrades purchased without approval.
