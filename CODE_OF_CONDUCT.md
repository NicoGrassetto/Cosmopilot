# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all contributors, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Our Standards

We expect all community members to:

- **Be respectful** - Treat all people with dignity and respect
- **Be inclusive** - Welcome diverse perspectives and experiences
- **Be constructive** - Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be professional** - Keep discussions focused and on-topic
- **Be honest** - Acknowledge mistakes and work to improve

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech of any kind
- Offensive comments related to gender, identity, religion, disability, race, or other characteristics
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Unwanted sexual attention or advances
- Threats or violence
- Publishing private information without consent
- Other conduct that creates an unsafe or unwelcoming environment

## Reporting Issues

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior:

1. Report directly to the project maintainers via private message
2. Email: Include details, context, and witnesses
3. All reports are confidential

## Enforcement

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:
- Warning or removal from discussions
- Temporary or permanent ban from the project
- Legal action if applicable

## Acknowledgment

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.0, and applies to all community spaces including GitHub, discussions, issues, and related channels.

Thank you for helping make Cosmopilot a welcoming community!
